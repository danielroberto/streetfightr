How We Fight the Streets

Navigation just like any other maps application

We aim to simplify the drive, therefore we use a similar mapping API as other well known navigation applications. Making sure the map is updated, accurate, and get you from point A to point B as fast as possible is our goal.

Work together

Our platform is built on crowdsourcing and data collection, because as a community of car enthusiasts, we should look out for each other. Whether its road hazards, speed bumps, and sharp inclines, users will build the database for the maps to integrate and notify drivers of known possible obstacles. Users can place warnings of road hazards for other drivers and describe roads for future drivers. Advanced options include describing types of obstacles, heights, and much more.

Driving, customized to you

Create profiles based on your cars. If your car is slammed to the ground, Streetfightr will calculate and find you a route best suited to the ride and height of your vehicle, based on the warnings created by the community.
